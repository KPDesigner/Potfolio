Project 1 Name : Smart Study Guide

Abstract:

Our project is a website based on R.T.M.N.U in which we will provide that whole Semester Syllabus with content . We try to arrange provided data according to the syllabus and provide video link related to every topic. It is very difficult task for students to find out and study those topic they missed at college due to some reason. We agree that, now a days whole information is available on Internet, but students are confused that which data is efficient for university based exam. In our project we refer textbook, reference book as per R.T.M.N.U and trusted video links which can helps students to study better and efficient.

The outcome of our project will possibly reduce the effort and crucial time of students who are searching and surfing over Internet for perfect data. The further implementation of this project can be used for whole college and every department. Once the initial modules get completed and is accepted by the society.

Project 2 Name : Transporter

Abstract:

In recent years, the development of worldwide transport has been outstanding. At the same time, due to the fast development of the transporter business, it is important to offer customer an enhanced travel platform. In order to support customer to provide service in various ways, online product is essential. During the research process for completing this work, a mobile transporter guide application was try to developing according to the existing condition, which is based on an existing Developed application

The objective of this project was try to develop a mobile transporter guide application with added functions will reduce the straggle for customer . The main goal of the application is to provide a wide variety of functions and common platform to benefit of customer. Moreover, this application aims to reach the right target group, and do real-time data analysis.
